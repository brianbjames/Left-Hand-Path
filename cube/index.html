Cube is an experimental electronic music project from Adam Keith of Oakland, California.

Musically, he touches on industrial, dub, techno, noise, and more, but does so with a distinctly personal, emotional touch — the spirit of a singer-songwriter lurks underneath his waves of distortion.

My Cube is Cube's first vinyl LP and the first release on Left Hand Path — it is a multifaceted, kaleidoscopic listen, containing a multitude of moods and atmospheres.
